"Command PhaseScriptExecution failed with a nonzero exit code . Flutter /bin/sh: /packages/flutter_tools/bin/xcode_backend.sh: No such file or directory"问题处理

2022 年 11 月 10 日

1.flutter module的目录最好和你的工程同目录

2.在Build Settings里 搜索FLUTTER，看下FLUTTER_APPLICATION_PATH 以及FLUTTER_ROOT的路径对不对

3.Flutter.xcconfig里flutter module的路径是否正确
